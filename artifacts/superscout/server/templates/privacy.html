Privacy Policy

Version 1.0

1. Who We Are

Data Controller: Paul Insley, trading as SuperScout, a sole trader registered in England, United Kingdom.

Email: privacy@superscout.pro

2. Data We Collect

We collect: email address, display name, FPL Manager ID, preferences, notification settings, device type, OS version, app version, and subscription status. Usage data (screen views, feature interactions, session duration) is collected only with your consent.

We do not collect special category data, FPL login credentials, real names, location/GPS data, contacts, health data, or photos.

3. How We Use Data and Our Lawful Basis

  • Providing the service and managing your account: Contract (Art. 6(1)(b)).
  • Personalised AI recommendations, analysis, and commentary: Legitimate interests (Art. 6(1)(f)).
  • Push notifications: Consent (Art. 6(1)(a)) — withdraw any time via app settings.
  • Analytics and product improvement: Consent (Art. 6(1)(a)) — opt-in only.
  • Financial record-keeping: Legal obligation (Art. 6(1)(c)) — HMRC requirements.
  • Preventing misuse and ensuring security: Legitimate interests (Art. 6(1)(f)).

4. Automated Decision-Making and Profiling

SuperScout uses AI (including large language models by Anthropic) to generate personalised recommendations based on your FPL data and preferences. This constitutes automated profiling. All recommendations are advisory — you retain full control over your decisions. You have the right to request human review of any automated output by contacting privacy@superscout.pro.

5. Who We Share Data With

Supabase (database, London UK), Anthropic (AI analysis), RevenueCat (subscriptions), Apple/Google (app distribution and payments), Beehiiv (email newsletters), Expo (push notifications).

We do not sell your data or share it for third-party marketing.

6. International Transfers

Some processors are based outside the UK. All transfers are protected by UK International Data Transfer Agreements (IDTA) or the UK Extension to the EU-US Data Privacy Framework.

7. How Long We Keep Your Data

  • Account data: until deletion + 30 days.
  • FPL data and recommendation history: current season + 2 previous.
  • Analytics data: rolling 12 months, then anonymised.
  • Financial records: up to 6 years (HMRC).
  • Push tokens: until you uninstall or revoke consent.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to: access, rectify, erase, restrict processing, data portability, object to processing, withdraw consent, and rights related to automated decision-making.

We respond within one calendar month. Contact: privacy@superscout.pro.

9. Children's Privacy

Not intended for children under 13. Users aged 13–17 may use SuperScout with parental permission. We are mindful of the ICO Age Appropriate Design Code.

10. Data Breach Notification

We will notify the ICO within 72 hours of becoming aware of a breach that poses a risk to your rights. We will inform you without undue delay where the breach is likely to result in high risk.

11. Security

We use encryption in transit (TLS) and at rest, secure access-controlled infrastructure, and regular security review. No method is 100% secure.

12. Changes

We may update this policy. Significant changes: 30 days notice via app or email.
