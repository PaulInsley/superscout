## PROMPT 6a — Privacy Policy Integration

REPLIT AGENT PROMPT — SuperScout Privacy Policy Setup

CONTEXT: SuperScout needs a privacy policy before any public launch or beta testing. The privacy policy will be hosted at superscout.pro/privacy. We’re using iubenda (iubenda.com) to generate and host the policy, then embedding or linking to it from the app.

TASK: Set up the privacy policy integration in the app:

1. ADD a privacy policy link to the Settings screen (app/(tabs)/settings.tsx):
- Add a “Privacy Policy” row in the settings list that opens the privacy policy URL in the device browser
- Use expo-web-browser to open the link: Linking.openURL(“https://superscout.pro/privacy”)
- Position it near the bottom of the settings screen, above the app version number
1. ADD a privacy policy checkbox to the onboarding email/signup screen:
- On the sign-up screen, add a checkbox: “I agree to the Privacy Policy and Terms of Service”
- The text “Privacy Policy” and “Terms of Service” should be tappable links that open in browser
- The sign-up button should be disabled until the checkbox is ticked
- Store consent in the consent_events Supabase table with event_type “privacy_accepted”
1. ADD a privacy policy link to the Paywall component (components/Paywall.tsx):
- Below the restore purchases link, add small text: “By subscribing you agree to our Privacy Policy and Terms of Service” with tappable links
1. UPDATE the landing page (server/templates/landing-page.html):
- Add a footer link to the privacy policy

The actual privacy policy content will be generated via iubenda’s dashboard separately — this task is about making sure the app links to it correctly from all the right places and captures consent.

URLs to use:

- Privacy Policy: https://superscout.pro/privacy
- Terms of Service: https://superscout.pro/terms

CONTEXT: SuperScout needs proper Expo and EAS configuration before we can create test builds for iOS and Android. The current app.json is minimal and missing key fields needed for app store submission and RevenueCat integration.

1. UPDATE app.json with the following:
- Add iOS bundle identifier: “pro.superscout.app”
- Add Android package name: “pro.superscout.app”
- Add iOS buildNumber: “1”
- Add Android versionCode: 1
- Add to plugins array: “react-native-purchases” (required for RevenueCat)
- Add to plugins array: [“expo-media-library”] (required for squad card saving)
