# REPLIT AGENT PROMPT — SuperScout: Restore Fixture Ticker on OUT Players

## CONTEXT

We recently removed the fixture ticker from OUT players on Transfer Advisor cards to reduce clutter. That was a mistake — the side-by-side comparison between OUT and IN fixtures was actually one of the most powerful visual elements on the card. When you can see red pills on the left (player you’re selling) next to green pills on the right (player you’re buying), the recommendation makes instant visual sense without reading anything.

## TASK

### 1. Restore the fixture ticker on OUT players

Add the FixtureTicker component back to the OUT player on every transfer card — both single swaps and package transfers.

The layout for each swap row should be:

**OUT side (left):**

- Player name
- Team · Price
- NEXT 5 ticker

**Arrow in the middle**

**IN side (right):**

- Player name
- Team · Price
- NEXT 5 ticker

Both tickers should sit at the same vertical position so they line up horizontally. This makes the fixture comparison scannable at a glance — red on the left, green on the right tells the whole story.

### 2. Make both tickers slightly more compact

To keep the card from getting too tall, reduce the pill size slightly on both OUT and IN tickers:

- Pill width: ~35px (down from whatever it is now)
- Pill font size: 10px
- Pill height: keep the same as current
- Gap between pills: 2px
- Remove the “NEXT 5” label text above the ticker — the pills are self-explanatory and the label was taking up space. If you feel it needs a label, use a single small “Fixtures” label in text-muted colour, but only if it fits without adding a new line.

### 3. Keep all other recent fixes

- Keep the DGW slash pill format (e.g. “TOT/CHE” with DGW label)
- Keep the player name spacing fix on package transfers
- Keep the ticker on Captain Picker cards as-is

### 4. Do NOT change anything else

- Do NOT modify the ticker colours or FDR mapping
- Do NOT modify the cache, staleness check, or AI generation
- Do NOT change card content (upside, risk, commentary, confidence badges)
