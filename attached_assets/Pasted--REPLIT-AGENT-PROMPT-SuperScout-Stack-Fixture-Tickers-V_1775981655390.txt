# REPLIT AGENT PROMPT — SuperScout: Stack Fixture Tickers Vertically on Transfer Cards

## CONTEXT

The fixture tickers on Transfer Advisor cards currently sit side-by-side — OUT player ticker on the left, IN player ticker on the right. The problem is that 5 pills per side don’t fit on a phone screen, causing the right-hand pills to get cut off. The DGW combined pills (e.g. “ARS/BUR”) make it worse because they’re wider than normal pills.

The fix: stack the tickers vertically instead of side-by-side. Each ticker gets the full card width, so all 5 fixtures fit comfortably.

## TASK

### 1. Change the Transfer Advisor card layout for tickers

Currently the OUT player info and IN player info sit side-by-side with tickers underneath each. Change the ticker section so that:

- The OUT player’s ticker shows on its own full-width row with a small “OUT” label in red (text-muted size) on the left
- The IN player’s ticker shows on the next full-width row below it with a small “IN” label in green on the left
- Both tickers show 5 fixtures at full compact pill size (35px width, 10px font as currently set)
- The two rows should be close together with minimal vertical gap (4-6px) so they feel like one comparison block

The visual effect should be: a row of red/amber pills on top, a row of green pills below. The contrast between the two rows tells the fixture story at a glance.

### 2. Keep the player name and price layout as-is

The OUT player name/team/price on the left and IN player name/team/price on the right should NOT change. Only the ticker rows move to a stacked full-width layout below the player info section.

### 3. Do NOT change Captain Picker cards

Captain Picker cards only have one ticker per card and they already look good. Leave them exactly as they are — full size pills with the “NEXT 5” label.

### 4. Do NOT change anything else

- Do NOT modify the ticker colours, FDR mapping, or DGW slash format
- Do NOT modify the cache, staleness check, or AI generation
- Do NOT change card content (upside, risk, commentary, confidence badges)
- Do NOT change the pill sizes — keep them at 35px compact as currently built
